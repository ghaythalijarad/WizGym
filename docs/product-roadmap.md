# Product Roadmap

## Phase 1: MVP (10-12 weeks)
- Auth and onboarding for all roles
- Membership plans, purchase, renewal, and freeze
- Booking engine for classes and PT sessions
- Trainer client list and basic plans
- Owner dashboard (attendance, revenue, retention)

## Phase 2: Growth (6-8 weeks)
- In-app role-based chat
- Nutrition plans and habit tracking
- Referral campaigns and promo engine
- Advanced reporting and branch comparisons

## Phase 3: Intelligence (6-8 weeks)
- AI-assisted plan recommendations
- Churn prediction and proactive retention workflows
- Smart scheduling suggestions
- Wearables insights and adaptive goals
